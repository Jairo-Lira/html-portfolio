Jairo's Movie Ranking


In this list I will be ranking my top 5 favorite movies of all time. I will tell you a little bit about what the movie is about and perhaps some interesting facts about it.

  • #1 Whiplash


    • In this movie we get to know Andrew Neiman (Miles Teller), a young student drummer whose career changes after crossing paths with Terrence Fletcher (J.K. Simmons). Andy pushes himself beyond his limits improving as a musician but becomes obsesed with being "one of the greats" as he is challenged by Fletcher on every competence they're in.

  • #2 Oppenheimer


    • Academy Award director Christopher Nolan tells us the story of J. Robert Oppenheimer in a way that navigates us through one of the events that changed human history. Cillian Murphy portrays this role in such a way that makes us feel empathy for Robert as he has moral regrets after leading his team in WW2.

  • #3 Green Book


    • It's hard for me to tell who the main protagonist is in this story since both Don Shirley and Tony Vallelonga are really interesting characters. In this journey we get to hear some incredible music and see a friendship be born between these two formidable characters.

  • #4 Jurassic Park


    • In this Steven Spielberg classic we see an adaptation of Michaels Chricton's novel in a way that leaves us terrified of facing one of these prehistoric creatures. We will root for every one of the characters and I'm sure you'll be amazed by how realistic dinosaurs look in this film.

  • #5 Back to the Future


    • "If you're going to build a time machine, why not to do it with style?". In this 80's classic we join Marty McFly and Doc. Emmet Brown in a journey through time on which they will have to repair some problems by changing some events in the past, but they'll soon find out that perhaps they changed more than they meant.
